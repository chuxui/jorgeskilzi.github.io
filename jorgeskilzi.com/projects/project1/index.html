Mosic
iOS Music Sport App
  • About the project

    Mosic. MOve your muSIC. It's an app that make gamification with users music and they movements. Encourage the user to do sport in a different way. Listen their music and make movements in the correct time to win more points and be the best Mosics with their songs.

  • Objective

    I wanted to experiment with a dark design and thinking in watch native application design from the beginning. Simple dark UI with light details for make an attractive design.

Mobile App

In usability is said that dark ui designs are negative because they can make mirror effect or for visually impaired. But I tested the app on multiple devices with Invision and the result is positive. Highlighted details make the app works in its entirety and darkness is not a problem at any moment.
